The Myth of 'Flyover Country'

'Flyover country' is a phrase derisively used to refer to the midwestern states of the U.S. It implies that there is little of value in these states, few tourist attractions or points of interest.
But how true is it? Do people really fly from coast to coast avoiding these huge swathes of land?
